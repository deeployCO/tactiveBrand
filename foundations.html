Shadows.

Three soft, low-blur shadows plus a punchy --shadow-pink for primary CTA hover. Never combine shadows on one element. No inner shadows in product UI.

--shadow-1rest
--shadow-2raised

05

Motion.

Fast, ease-out, mostly. Page reveals 360ms slide-up + fade. No bounces. No springs. Loading is a single pink dot pulsing — not spinners.

DURATION
120ms · 200ms · 360ms

Fast / base / slow. 120ms for nudges, 200ms for state changes, 360ms for page reveals.

EASING
cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)

House ease-out. Punchy in, settles smoothly. Used for almost everything.

LOADING
pulsing pink dot

A single pink dot pulsing at 1.4s. Echoes the logo. Never spinners.

01

Logo.

Lowercase wordmark in deep navy with a single hot-pink dot. The dot is the brand mark — it can stand alone as a status indicator, a section period, or a favicon.

Wordmark · primary
Wordmark · inverse tactive.
Mark · 56px
Mark · 28px
Mark · 12px (status)
DO

Always lowercase. The wordmark ends with a pink period — it's the brand mark, not punctuation. Treat it as part of the lockup.

DO

Echo the dot in major headlines: end the line with a small pink period. Sparingly — once per section is plenty.

DON'T

Capitalize as Tactive in the lockup. In flowing body copy, capitalized Tactive is fine.

DON'T

Recolor the dot. The pink is fixed — #E91A4F. No gradients, no shadows, no rotation.
